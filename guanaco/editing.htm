Editorial Services


With over a decade's experience in book retail, corporate copywriting, academia and publishing, we provide expert and efficient editing, proofreading and copywriting services for all needs. Promotional, academic, business, technical or creative, we offer individual attention and quick turnaround.


Copy-Editing:
Copy-editing for sense, clarity, accuracy and structure, as well as proofing for grammar, spelling and consistency. The depth of the edit ranges from basic to substantive depending on the text and your needs.
R300 per hour (usually 750 to 1500 words).

Proofreading:
Proofreading for grammar, spelling and consistency. Rates depend on the complexity of the text.
R300 per hour (usually 2000 to 4000 words).

Manuscript assessment:
We consider your manuscript or project proposal with a publisher's eye, supply a written report on its strengths and weaknesses, and recommend changes or possible publishers.
From R2000 per 80,000 word manuscript.

Copywriting:
Product reviews, book blurbs, website content. We have specific experience in publishing and retail marketing, promotional copy and magazine editorial.
From R300 per hour.

Typesetting:
We set and design your book, report or catalogue to specification with sophisticated graphic design elements. We set in InDesign and produce best-quality PDFs.
Around R35 per page, including basic graphics and illustrations.
