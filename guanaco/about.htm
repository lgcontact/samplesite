About Guanaco.co.za


Guanaco.co.za was established by Louis Greenberg in 2005 and works with individual, academic and corporate clients and organisations. Louis's client list includes Random House Struik, Pan Macmillan, Penguin Books South Africa, Sharp Sharp Media, NB Publishers, Exclusive Books, English Studies in Africa, Faradawn, Margie Orford, Fiona Snyckers, Richard de Nooy, Robyn Goss, Khanya-aicdd, Wits University, and Tshikululu Social Investments which manages CSI funds for major corporates including Anglo American and FirstRand.

Louis is a full member of the Professional Editors' Group and ANFASA and subscribes to their codes of practice.

With a wealth of experience in book retail, publishing, print and web design, marketing and promotion, Louis offers editorial, web and print design services with an individual and expert touch, working efficiently, approachably, and time- and cost-effectively.
